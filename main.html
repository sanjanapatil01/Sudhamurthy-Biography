About

Sudha Murthy is one of those rare women whose life story continues to inspire millions. A writer, philanthropist, and entrepreneur, she has made an indelible mark on Indian society through her contributions in various fields. Known for her prolific writing, advocacy for education for underprivileged children, and her pivotal role in the success of Infosys, India’s leading IT company, Sudha Murthy’s work transcends borders. Dressed simply in traditional Indian attire, her philanthropic efforts have earned her global recognition and respect.

From becoming India’s first female engineer to making a mark at Infosys, Sudha Murthy’s relentless commitment to social change and education has played a significant role in shaping her journey. Let’s delve into Sudha Murthy’s educational background and her extraordinary achievements

Sudha Murthy, a name that resonates with millions across the globe, is known for her remarkable achievements in the fields of social work, literature, and business. From humble beginnings to becoming a prominent philanthropist and an influential figure in Indian society, Sudha Murthy’s life is a testament to resilience, compassion, and determination. Here are a few important details about Sudha Murthy and her early life:

  • On 19 August 1950, Sudha Murthy was born in a Brahmin family in Shiggaon, Karnataka.
  • Her father Dr R.H. Kulkarni who was a surgeon and her mother Vimala Kulkarni supported her in her endeavours right from her childhood and raised young Sudha along with her three siblings.
  • With constant support from her family in childhood, Sudha Murthy went on to pursue unconventional career choices which were not popular among girls.
  • An educated atmosphere in the family instilled in her a passion to do something extraordinary at an early age.
  • Sudha Murthy’s brother Srinivas Kulkarni is a renowned astronomer who received the Dan David Prize in 2017.
  • Her early life experiences and affinity for her grandmother became the foundation for some of her books.
  • Sudha Murthy’s childhood is often characterized by simplicity and a fire to learn.
  • Due to her love for literature, she was often found immersed in literature.

Sudha Murthy’s education has a major role to play in shaping her as a successful author, even though her educational path was more technical. Her hard work and commitment are clear from the fact that she emerged as a topper during her bachelor’s and master’s degrees

Sudha Murthy completed her BE in Electrical Engineering from B.V.B College of Engineering, Hubli, where she won a gold medal for her exceptional academic performance. Later on, she considered the option for higher studies and went to pursue her ME in Computer Science in 1974 from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, and later received a gold medal from the then Karnataka chief minister for topping both the final exams.

Achievements

  • Pioneering Engineer:

    Sudha Murthy earned her BE in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from B.V.B. College of Engineering & Technology (now KLE Technological University) and later pursued ME in Computer Science from the Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore.

  • Trailblazer at TELCO:

    She became the first female engineer to work at TATA Engineering and Locomotive Company (TELCO)

  • Pioneering Engineer:

    Infosys Foundation: As the chairperson of the Infosys Foundation, Sudha Murthy has contributed immensely to education, public health, rural development, and arts and culture. The foundation has built libraries, schools, and hospitals, and provided disaster relief in India.

  • Public Healthcare:

    Collaborating with the Gates Foundation, she has worked on improving healthcare facilities and providing vaccinations.

  • Prolific Writer:

    Sudha Murthy has authored several books in English and Kannada, addressing various genres including children’s literature, novels, and non-fiction

  • Member of Parliament, Rajya Sabha (2024):

    Nominated for her work in social causes and education, contributing to policy-making and public welfare.

  • Education Initiatives:

    Beyond the Infosys Foundation, she has supported countless students through scholarships and mentoring, helping shape future generations.

  • Arts and Culture:

    Sudha Murthy has also been instrumental in preserving Indian heritage and promoting cultural activities
